Consent to Participate

Thank you for participating in our study for COGS 164! Before beginning, please read the following information.

This study examines how different types of music may influence learning and memory. We will have you complete a word learning task while listening to music or in silence. This will happen over three different trials. Each trial will have a completely new set of words.

Important: To ensure accurate results, please complete this experiment in a quiet, distraction-free environment. Avoid multitasking and minimize background noise. Please use headphones if you are able.

This study will take about 15 to 20 minutes and will require your full attention.

Your participation is entirely voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time without penalty. If at any point during the study you wish to not participate anymore, you may close the window. Possible risks include boredom and fatigue while doing the study.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information and your responses will remain confidential. Responses will be used only for research purposes within the COGS 164 class.

If you have any questions concerning this study please reach out to nalbahar@ucsd.edu or zalcala@ucsd.edu.

By checking the box below and clicking Start Experiment, you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age, have read and understood this information, and agree to participate in our study.
